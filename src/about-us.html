Transforming Ideas into Market-Leading Products

A dynamic space where visionary minds are empowered to innovate, collaborate, and transform groundbreaking ideas into thriving businesses, driving the future of entrepreneurship.

The Government of India encourages the setup of incubation centers so that ample opportunities can be given to young entrepreneurs. Accordingly, Bhopal Smart City Development Corporation came forward with the initiative to set up B-Nest, the Bhopal Incubation Center. B-Nest is a subsidiary of BSCDCL. At B-Nest, we create a culture of excellence and nurture young entrepreneurs by providing them with mentorship and the assistance needed to establish their entrepreneurial dreams. B-Nest is an innovation house for budding start-ups in the city, offering the best infrastructure and services to help promote their business ideas and turn start-ups into multinational organizations.

Mission

To create a ecosystem where Start-ups can Innovate, Create & Succeed with technological assistance & guidance from Industry experts.

Vision

To promote entrepreneurial spirit amongst youth, researchers, engineers and society at large by promoting cutting edge information technology start-ups which will lead to economic and social development of Bhopal.
